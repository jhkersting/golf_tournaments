Rounds

Team leaderboard by round uses sum of Top X for player-based formats. Team Best Ball applies Top X per hole; singles/shamble apply Top X on round totals. Full-tournament weighted team totals use average of Top X. All two-man formats use sum of groups for that round and average of groups in weighted totals. Two man scramble uses one score per pair and 35% / 15% pair handicaps when enabled, capped at the pair's low handicap. Two man shamble uses both player scores and 80% of each player's handicap when enabled. Two man best ball uses the best gross or net hole score within each pair. Scramble uses fixed team scoring in both views, with team handicap capped at the team's low handicap. Individuals always show each player's own (weighted) total for that view.
